How to play Pig Dice:

  • Players take turns rolling the dice or holding.
    • Choose to Roll the dice:
      • - The number rolled gets added to the player's Round total.
      • - However, if a 1 is rolled that player's turn is over, and their round total score is lost.
    • Choose to Hold:
      • - The players Round Total is added to their Total score.
      • - This also ends that player's turn.
  • Roll as many times as you like, but risk losing that round's points. 1st player to 100 points wins.
